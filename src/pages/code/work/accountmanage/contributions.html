Other Considerations

You may make 3 contribution changes every 30 days, with one contribution change allowed every 7 days.

You may contribute from 1% to 50% of your eligible earnings on a before-tax and/or after-tax basis, subject to IRS limits.

Total contributions for before-tax and after-tax eligible earnings may not exceed 50%.

You may increase or decrease your contribution rate at any time.

Your contribution rate change will take effect as soon as administratively practicable.

You may change your investment allocation for future contributions at any time (only whole percentages are permitted).

Rollovers from another employer's qualified retirement plan or from a conduit individual retirement account/ annuity (IRA) are accepted immediately upon hire.

If you will be age 50 or older this year, and are eligible to make before-tax contributions, you may elect to make catch-up contributions from eligible earnings from 1% to 100%. Catch-up contributions are not eligible for company match.
