Andretti vs Andretti at Long Beach

The 1986 CART race in Long Beach, California produced the first career win for Michael Andretti. In the race, he competed against his father, Mario Andretti, who was by then a seasoned veteran. The painting depicts a battle between the father and son during that race, along the main straightaway, with Michael in front in the #18 Kraco Racing car, and Mario in back in the #5 Newman/Haas racing car. I was fortunate to see a Formula E race at Long Beach in April 2015 and have loved that street circuit ever since. In this painting, I tried to depict the audience grandstands as an abstract mix of colors. This was one of the first paintings I did after completing my Bird Book.
