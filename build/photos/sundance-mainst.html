Sundance 2015: Evening on Main Street

In January 2015, Amanda and I returned to the Sundance Film Festival for the 4th time. We originally met in Park City, UT at the 2009 festival, returned again in 2010 and 2011, and were engaged to be married at Sundance 2015.
