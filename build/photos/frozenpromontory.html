Frozen Bench at Promontory Point

This was the first HDR image I ever created. At the time, I was working for the University of Chicago and one freezing cold winter day I walked out to Promontory Point. I had been wanting to experiment with HDR and had seen it used with great results on photos of snow and ice. Since I first posted this image to Flickr, it has consistently been one of my most highly-viewed photos.
