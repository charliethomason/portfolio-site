Open Range: Saddleback Mesa (book cover)

I used this photo for the cover of my first book, Open Range: From Monument Valley to the Mojave Desert. The rock formation in the photo is called Saddleback Mesa. I took this photo just after midnight on the outskirts of Monument Valley. I chose this photo as the cover of the book because of its compositional symmetry and color balance. I also named the book "Open Range" because of the road sign in the photo.
