About Charlie Thomason

My name is Charlie Thomason and I'm a Front-end Web Developer and Artist living in Chicago, Illinois. I love photography, auto racing, movies, traveling, birds, and playing guitar and piano. This website features some of my best paintings and photos, and my blog.

I was born in Red Bank, New Jersey in 1984, went to high school & college in Kentucky, and moved to Chicago in 2008. I currently live in the South Loop neighborhood of Chicago with my wife, Amanda, and our 2 cats, Baxter and Chloe.

My specialties as a Web Developer include web accessibility, responsive CSS, cross-browser compatibility, and JavaScript/jQuery. I am currently employed full-time as a Presentation Layer Engineer for Razorfish, where I’ve been happily coding since January 2013. I received my Masters in New Media Studies from DePaul University in 2010, and studied web development at The Starter League in early 2012.

I've always been a visually creative person, and earned my Bachelor of Fine Arts in Art Studio from the University of Kentucky in 2007. Today, I still enjoy doing art as a hobby. I especially enjoy working with watercolor and ink painting, drawing, and photography. In 2012, I self-published a photography book called Open Range. Since 2014, I've been working on paintings of birds of the Chicagoland area.

About this website

Although this website does not feature a true portfolio of my work as a Web Developer, I hope that the website itself will function as an example of my front-end coding skills. The entire site I designed and built from scratch. The content is all static HTML, built using Handlebars, Assemble, Grunt, jQuery, and Sass. The main site is hosted with Github Pages, and the blog is hosted on Tumblr using a custom Tumblr theme. If you want to see more of how the site is built, feel free to view the source code for this website.
