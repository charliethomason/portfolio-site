Artist & Web Developer from Chicago

Charlie Thomason is a Front-end Web Developer and Artist living in Chicago, Illinois. He is currently a Presentation Layer Engineer for Razorfish, where he specializes in web accessibility, responsive CSS, JavaScript/jQuery, and cross-browser compatibility. As an Artist he loves painting, drawing, and photography. Charlie also loves auto racing, movies, birds, and pizza. This website features a gallery of his best art, a full bio, and a personal Tumblr blog.
